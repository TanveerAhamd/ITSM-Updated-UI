Our Mission

At TEF | ITSM, we keep government school computer labs running smoothly through regular maintenance, installations, networking, and technical support. Our goal is to create a reliable digital learning environment for every student.

Watch the Video

Lab Maintenance

We conduct monthly visits to ensure all computer systems and lab equipment are running efficiently and remain in top condition.

Installations & Setup

We handle installation and configuration of systems, software, and devices to set up fully functional and optimized computer labs.

Electricity & Power Issues

Our team troubleshoots and resolves power-related problems to ensure uninterrupted operation of computers and networking devices.

Networking & NOC Labs

We manage and maintain networks and NOC-based computing labs (X550, M300, and system-based) ensuring seamless connectivity and performance.
