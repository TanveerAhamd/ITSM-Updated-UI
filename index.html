About us

Keeping teams productive with dependable IT support

TEF|ITSM supports Tareen Education Foundation initiatives with responsive help desk, on-site assistance, and secure, well-maintained systems.

From classrooms to offices, we deliver proactive monitoring, rapid incident resolution, and lifecycle management so your teams can focus on impact.

  • Proactive monitoring & maintenance
  • Security & data protection

Our Services

At TEF | ITSM, we help government schools keep their computer labs fully functional. We provide monthly visits, free maintenance, installation services, complaint handling, networking, and support for NOC computing labs (X550, M300, and system-based labs).

Watch the Video

Lab Maintenance

Monthly visits to check and maintain computer labs, keeping everything functional and smooth.

Installations & Setup

Setting up systems, software, and lab equipment for fully functional labs.

Electricity & Power Issues

Handling power problems and ensuring uninterrupted electricity for labs and computers.

Networking & NOC Labs

Maintaining networks and NOC computing labs (X550, M300, and system-based labs) for smooth operations.
